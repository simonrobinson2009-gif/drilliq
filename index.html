No. You get a single HTML file. Open it in any browser on your phone, tablet or laptop. It works completely offline — no internet needed on match day or at training.
Is this suitable if I've never coached before? +
Yes — that's exactly who it's written for. All the coaching notes use plain everyday language. No qualifications needed, no jargon. If you know roughly what a wall pass is, you have enough background to use this pack confidently.
What age group is this for? +
The drills and coaching notes are written for U8 and U10 players — roughly ages 7 to 10. The movements are simple enough for beginners but still useful as players develop. More age group packs are planned.
Can I use it on my phone during training? +
That's exactly how it's designed to be used. The layout is mobile-first — large text, big tap targets, easy to navigate with one hand. Save the file to your home screen and it opens like an app.

Yes. The U8/U10 pack is being built toward 20 drills. Everyone who buys now gets all future drills added to their pack at no extra cost.
